Leicestershire

Eyebrook Res

2 juv Whinchats on Leics side of the inflow near the fence, also Cattle Egret still around the inflow. Wader totals: 5 Black-tailed Godwits, 13 Ruff, 1 Dunlin, 1 Redshank & 6 Common Sandpipers. Female Red-crested Pochard still between the south-west corner and the dam. NB reports on the national news services of a Curlew Sandpiper today and yesterday are assumed to refer to a rather long-billed Dunlin, unless anyone has photographic evidence to the contrary. The bird present (and photographed) early on Friday morning was not seen again that day, and hasn’t been seen since by any of the Eyebrook regulars despite plenty of people looking.
