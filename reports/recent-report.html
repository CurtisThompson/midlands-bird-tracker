Oakthorpe

Yellow-browed Warbler (but NB appears to show some pro-Hume’s characters from photos and has not been heard to call, so the ID is not confirmed) along the Nature Trail south-east of the village. Limited parking on Coronation Lane, or park in the small car park by the church in Donisthorpe and follow the path south for c 1 mile.
